About Me

My name is Stuart Schafer, unless I’m in trouble or did something wrong. In that case, my name is Rudy Gesegnet. I have lived my entire life in the state of North Carolina. I was born in Fayetteville, NC, aka Fayet-nam, Fay-Ruit, and Bragg-dad. I went to school at Appalachian State in Boone, NC, and then UNC Charlotte. I met my wonderful, beautiful, smart, amazing, talented, gorgeous wife in Charlotte, NC, and we were married on my birthday (I better not forget our anniversary!).

I’ve worked in Charlotte for the past 17 years mostly in inside sales, tech support, and customer service. We moved to Cary for a great job opportunity for my wife, and it made sense for me to go back to school to pursue another passion; computer coding. I am so eager to learn and code to create and bring my ideas to life. I enjoy most sports, love the UNC Tarheels, Carolina Panthers, and of course my alma mater, ASU Mountaineers. I love to visit places I’ve never been and experience the culture. When not coding, you can often find me playing video games or Netflixing. We also have 2 adorable furry critters who rule our house; Socks (L) and Buffy (R). (click on them to see a picture)
